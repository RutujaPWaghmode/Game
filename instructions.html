How to Play

  • A colored ball will drop from the top of the screen.
  • Click to rotate the basket and match the ball's color with the correct basket section.
  • Each correct match earns you 10 points.
  • Each incorrect match deducts 5 points and reduces your attempts.
  • You have 3 attempts per level. Use them wisely!
  • Reach 500 matches to advance to the next level, or complete within the time limit for a bonus!
  • If you run out of time or attempts, the game is over.
